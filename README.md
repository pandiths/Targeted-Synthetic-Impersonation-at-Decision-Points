# Targeted Synthetic Impersonation at Decision Points

## Overview
This repository hosts the research paper **"Targeted Synthetic Impersonation at Decision Points"**, which examines targeted synthetic impersonation attacks using text, voice, and video at high-stakes decision points.

The work focuses on real-world risks across enterprise environments, political processes, and electronic Know Your Customer (e-KYC) systems.

## Focus Areas
- Synthetic text, voice, and video impersonation
- Decision-point targeting strategies
- Enterprise and identity verification threats
- Defensive and mitigation approaches

## Paper 
`paper/Targeted_Synthetic_Impersonation_at_Decision_Points.pdf`

## Scope
This repository is **paper-only** and focuses on threat analysis and defense strategies.  
Implementation and tooling are considered future work.
